London, United Kingdom

The city of the Monarchy of the United Kingdom houses the famous Buckingham Palace. The modern yet old style of architecture of the city is very refreshing to see. Integrated technology in these buildings made the city interesting. Its underground subway system is also one of the best in the world.

How to get there

Apply for UK Visa and then book a flight to Heathrow airport
