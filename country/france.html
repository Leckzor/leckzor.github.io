Paris, France

The city of love is what they call it. This city is the center for art, fashion, and gastronomy. The historical landmarks that this city has to offer attracts tourist every year. The architecture of this city is very pleasing to the eye that you can look almost everywhere and see these intricate buildings and tempts you into taking a picture.
